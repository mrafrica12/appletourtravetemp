Everything you need to know before booking your Tanzania or Zanzibar adventure.

Most nationalities require a Tanzania tourist visa. Zanzibar is part of Tanzania, so the same visa applies. You can apply online at the Tanzania e-Visa portal. We offer visa assistance services to help you with the process.
Zanzibar is beautiful year-round. The best weather is June–October (dry season) and December–February. We recommend avoiding the main rainy seasons (April–May and November). Read our detailed seasonal guide on the blog.
