Our Story

Who We Are

Local Experts, Global Standards

Apple Tours & Safaris was founded in Stone Town, Zanzibar by a team of passionate local guides who believed that the best travel experiences come from those who truly know and love the place they're showing you.

What started as a small family operation offering dolphin-watching trips has grown into one of Zanzibar's most trusted travel companies — with safaris across mainland Tanzania, airport transfer networks, and hotel partnerships stretching from the Serengeti to the Indian Ocean.

Our guides grew up speaking Swahili, eating pilau rice from the night market, and watching sunsets over the dhow harbour. That lived knowledge is what we bring to every trip we design — and what our travellers feel the moment they arrive.
