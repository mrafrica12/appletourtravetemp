Why Zanzibar

Africa's Spice Island

Zanzibar is a UNESCO-listed archipelago off Tanzania's coast — a place where Arab trading dhows, Portuguese forts, Indian merchants and British colonizers left an extraordinary cultural imprint on a single tropical island.

🌊
Indian Ocean Beaches Powder-white sand and turquoise water stretching the entire coast
🌶️
Historic Spice Routes Once the world's largest cloves producer — still fragrant with cinnamon, nutmeg and vanilla
🐠
Marine Biodiversity Spinner dolphins, sea turtles, whale sharks, and vibrant coral reefs
