Why Tanzania

Africa's Greatest Wildlife Destination

Tanzania is home to some of Africa's most iconic national parks. The Serengeti, Ngorongoro, Tarangire, Lake Manyara, and Mount Kilimanjaro — each one extraordinary, each one different.

🐘
Serengeti National Park Home to the Great Migration — 1.5 million wildebeest in motion
🦏
Ngorongoro Crater World's largest caldera with the highest density of Big Five
⛰️
Mount Kilimanjaro The roof of Africa — Africa's highest and most climbable peak
